Kuran Sayfası

🇬🇧 English:

Hello!
My name is Bahadir, and my email address is bbatca@gmail.com.
I wanted to first show the translation of the verses, followed by the original Arabic text. This way, our readers can understand the meaning while reading the Arabic text. I believe that reading in this way will positively impact language learning.
Note:
The translations were obtained as SQL data from tanzil.net.
In the applied filters: punctuation marks like "secavend", fine pronunciation of "seen", "vasıl nun", "med-kasr", and many others were applied, while hemze pronunciation marks were not applied. Hemze with diacritics is read, while those without are already considered elongation letters. Symbols like hizb and sajdah on the side pages were not used. Please do not hesitate to report any errors you notice to us.

I de tillämpade filtren: skiljetecken som "secavend", fin uttal av "seen", "vasıl nun", "med-kasr" och många andra tillämpades, medan hemze uttalsmarkeringar inte tillämpades. Hemze med diakritiska tecken läses, medan de utan redan betraktas som förlängningsbokstäver. Symboler som hizb och sajdah på sidans marginaler användes inte.
